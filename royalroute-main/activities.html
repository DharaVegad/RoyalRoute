RIVERRAFTING

Despite how it seems, mountaineers are far from the only adventures seeking excitement in the country. The diverse setting and stretches of the waterways are ideal for river rafting.

Moutain Biking

With the mountain bike on spectacular single trails through the Alps, along the hills of the Jura from Basel to Lake Geneva, or through the Alpine foothills and across the entire country of Switzerland.

Road Cycling

Superb roads, unspoilt scenery: Switzerland is a paradise for devotees of road cycling. Level cycling paths by lakes.

Ice Climbing

The Swiss Alpine Guides' mountain guides will show you the magnificent world of eternal ice and teach you how to use ropes, pick-axe and pitons.
